About Lagan Lutherie School

Lagan Lutherie School offers classes in guitar making for students at all levels from beginner to advanced.

Classes are under the tuition of Sam Irwin, a Luthier with 40 years experience. Full time, part time, evening and weekend classes are available. All equipment and tools are provided with guidance at every step, and assistance where necessary.

Be sure to check out details of our courses, our photos and videos of finished guitars and other instruments. Recently we have been joined by another master luthier who specialises in violins and archtops with the aim to offer courses in these disciplines. We’d be happy to hear from you if you’re interested.
