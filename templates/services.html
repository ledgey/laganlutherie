Our Services

Guitar Set-up

We offer professional guitar set-up services to ensure your instrument is optimized for playability and sound quality. Our skilled technicians can adjust your guitar's action, intonation, and more to your exact preferences.

Our experienced team can repair and restore a wide range of stringed instruments, including guitars, basses, violins, and more. From basic repairs to full restorations, we can help bring your instrument back to life.

Commissioned Instrument Building

Looking for a custom-built instrument? Our skilled luthiers can work with you to design and build a one-of-a-kind instrument to your exact specifications. From tonewoods to hardware, we'll make sure your dream instrument becomes a reality.
