About Lagan Lutherie School

Lagan Lutherie School offers classes in guitar making for students at all levels from beginner to advanced.

Classes are under the tuition of Sam Irwin, a Luthier with 40 years experience. Full time, part time, evening and weekend classes are available. All equipment and tools are provided with guidance at every step, and assistance where necessary.

Be sure to check out details of our courses, our photos and videos of finished guitars and other instruments. Recently we have been joined by another master luthier who specialises in violins and archtops with the aim to offer courses in these disciplines. We’d be happy to hear from you if you’re interested.

Courses

Built around you!

Sessions are between 3 and 4 hours and are available on Tues-Sat. Evening sessions are available Tuesday and Thursday. It takes approximately 100-150 hours to build a guitar, this varies based on the complexity and skill level.

Pay-As-You-Go

This is our most flexible option where you can work on your guitar building skills at your own pace. Sessions are between 3 and 4 hours and are available on Tues-Sat.

Full-Time Course

Focused Workshops

Join our focused workshops and learn how to improve your guitar building skills. Workshops are available on various topics, including guitar set-up and fret-dressing.

Additional Services

As well as expert tuition, our team of experienced luthiers are also available, contact us below for a free estimate.

Guitar Set-up

We offer professional guitar set-up services to ensure your instrument is optimized for playability and sound quality. Our skilled technicians can adjust your guitar's action, intonation, and more to your exact preferences.

Instrument Repair and Restoration

Our experienced team can repair and restore a wide range of stringed instruments, including guitars, basses, violins, and more. From basic repairs to full restorations, we can help bring your instrument back to life.

Commissioned Instrument Building

Looking for a custom-built instrument? Our skilled luthiers can work with you to design and build a one-of-a-kind instrument to your exact specifications. From tonewoods to hardware, we'll make sure your dream instrument becomes a reality.
